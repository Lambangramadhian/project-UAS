Major Service

Some of the components that will usually be checked or replaced if they are in poor condition include:

Benefits of Major Service

  • Engine oil, transmission, and gordan replaced with new ones
  • Oil filter also replaced
  • Cleaning of the air filter
  • Inspection of bolts on the vehicle's suspension
  • Checking spark plugs and ignition cables
  • Cleaning of the intake manifold
  • Adjustment of valves on vehicles with manual valves

Major Service Process

  • Inspection of the vehicle's brake condition
  • Clutch inspection
  • Radiator inspection
  • Inspection of the vehicle's electrical system
  • Checking the condition of the fuel system

Minor Service

About Us

Bengkel Putra Prima

Bengkel Putra Prima was established in 2010 with the aim of providing high quality motor vehicle repair and maintenance services. With a team of experienced mechanics and modern equipment, we are ready to provide the best solution for all your vehicle problems.

We are committed to providing the best service at an affordable price. Every mechanic at Bengkel Putra Prima has undergone specialized training and has a certification of competence in their respective fields.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To become the leading workshop in Indonesia that provides solutions for the maintenance and repair of motor vehicles with the highest quality standards and the best customer service.

Mission

  • Providing quality repair services at competitive prices
  • Utilizing the latest technology in every repair process
  • Ensuring customer satisfaction as our top priority
  • Developing professional and competent human resources
